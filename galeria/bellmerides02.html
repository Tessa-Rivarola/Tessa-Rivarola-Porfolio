"Bellmerides".

Solo dance and theatre directed by Marisol Salinas with the artistic assistance of Birgit Asshoff (Berlin) premiered in Berlin and Paderborn (Germany) in February 2015. In the same year, a presentation was made in Madrid (Spain) and 10 presentations at the Institute Cultural Paraguayan Alemán (ICPA) and in The Granel, Asunción.
