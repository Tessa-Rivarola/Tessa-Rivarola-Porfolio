"Ashes"

Dance theatre play about women in the war of 1870 and directed by Wal Mayans. Asunción, July 2009.
Participant in the International Theater Festival in Córdoba, Argentina, October 2009.
Participant in the celebrations for 100 years of the Jose Alencar Theater, Fortaleza, Brazil, July 2010.
Participant in the Asunción International Theater Festival (FITA), October 2010 and October 2011.
Presented at the Potlach Theater (Italy) in July 2012.
Participant in the International Festival MIRADA, Festival of Performing Arts in Santos, Brazil, in September 2012.
