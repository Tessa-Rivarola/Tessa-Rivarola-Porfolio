"Soria"

Dance theatre play on Mbya Guaraní myths about the creation of the universe, directed by Wal Mayans. Invited by Cia do Abracao, Curitiba, Brazil June 2008. Invited by the Jose Alencar Theater, Fortaleza, Brazil, December 2008. Participant of the representation of Paraguay in the International Fair of Shanghai, China, June 2010.
